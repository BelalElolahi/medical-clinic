King Hussein Cancer Center

The King Hussein Cancer Foundation The King Hussein Cancer Foundation (KHCF) is an independent, non-governmental, non-profit, national foundation founded in 2001 by Royal Decree and headed by HRH Princess Ghida Talal. HRH Princess Ghida presides over all management functions and operations of the King Hussein Cancer Foundation (KHCF) and Center (KHCC) along with a board of trustees comprised of a dedicated group of prominent volunteers who are experts in the fields of health, economy and finance.

Our Mission Providing the best comprehensive care for cancer patients in Jordan and the Middle East. Raising awareness about the importance of early detection and cancer prevention. Providing nonprofit cancer insurance for treatment at the KHCC. Performing scientific research for the purpose of lowering mortality rates and alleviating the suffering brought on by cancer. Fundraising to support KHCC’s mission.
